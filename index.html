About Us

In Khaling Khaja Ghar, we are passionate about creating a memorable dining experience for our customers. Our restaurant is committed to providing quality food, excellent service, and a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
Our menu features a variety of delicious Nepali and Indian dishes, made with the freshest ingredients and authentic spices. From our mouthwatering momos and thukpa, to our flavorful curries and biryanis, there is something for everyone to enjoy. We also offer a selection of vegetarian and vegan options, catering to all dietary needs.
Our team of experienced chefs and friendly staff are dedicated to ensuring that every visit to Khaling Khaja Ghar is a delightful one. Whether you're looking for a cozy dinner for two, a family gathering, or a corporate event, we have the expertise and resources to make your dining experience truly special. Thank you for considering Khaling Khaja Ghar for your next meal. We look forward to serving you soon!
